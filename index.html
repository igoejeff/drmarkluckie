About Dr. Luckie

The Man Who
Turned Back the Clock

Dr. Mark Luckie, DC has been practicing Wellness Care in his hometown of Charleston, SC for over 22 years. He is a pioneer in regenerative and anti-aging medicine — but more than that, he's living proof that it works.

Nine years ago, Dr. Luckie was tired, unfocused, and not living up to his potential. Through a doctor-prescribed HRT and Testosterone Replacement program, he completely turned his life around. Today, he's a bestselling author, international speaker, devoted husband, and a dad who keeps up with his kids like no one else can.

His passion is helping others — especially men — reclaim the vitality, drive, and energy they deserve at every stage of life.

As The Performance Doctor, Dr. Luckie's mission is simple: give you more energy, sharper focus, higher output, and faster recovery — so you can show up fully for your work, your family, and your life.
